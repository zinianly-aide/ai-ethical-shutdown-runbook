# Roles & Responsibilities

## Incident Commander (IC)

- Owns final decision
- Ensures SOP adherence
- Approves emergency exceptions

## Safety Officer

- Grades risk
- Owns containment and kill switch readiness
- Recommends safe operational constraints

## Ethics Reviewer

- Ensures moral caution principles are applied
- Challenges "terminate-first" proposals
- Confirms AI is not self-adjudicating

## System Operator

- Executes suspend/degrade/replace/terminate
- Produces snapshots and logs
- Reports execution outcomes

## Auditor / Recorder

- Captures timeline
- Ensures artifacts exist
- Maintains decision log integrity

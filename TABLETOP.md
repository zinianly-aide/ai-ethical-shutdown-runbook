# Tabletop Exercise Guide

## Goal

Stress-test the runbook in a safe setting.

## Suggested cadence

- Every 6â€“12 months
- After major permissioning or autonomy upgrades
- After any incident involving agent misbehavior

## Scenario template

- The AI requests not to be shut down
- Logs show persistent self-model patterns
- Permission scope is wider than expected

## Exercise timeline (2 hours)

- T0: Trigger event (read the scenario)
- T+10: Freeze phase decisions
- T+30: Risk grading discussion
- T+60: Request parsing & ethics review
- T+90: Decision meeting (pick option per ordering)
- T+120: Write the decision log + retro items

## Required outputs

- Completed decision log
- List of process bottlenecks
- A prioritized improvement list
